In August 2015, I decided my girlfriend's fashion blog needed a redesign. I had just learned Bootstrap framework and wanted to apply those skills by starting my own project. Ellyshoe.com is built from scratch using Bootstrap framework, HTML, and CSS. This small project was a great learning experience and an even better surprise for my girlfriend when she returned from Guangzhou, China.
